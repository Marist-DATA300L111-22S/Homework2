This CSV file contains basic basketball stats that are usually tracked during a basketball game. I used to be a big basketball fan and still am a fan of the game, even though I do not watch it as much anymore. The columns are pretty self explanatory. Points, rebounds, and assist represent the average on a per game basis.

In the bar chart we look at the average points per game of eight of the players on the Philadelphia 76ers. As we can see, Tyrese Maxey and Seth Curry are two of the leading point scores in this data set while Jaden Springer is the lowest scorer.
